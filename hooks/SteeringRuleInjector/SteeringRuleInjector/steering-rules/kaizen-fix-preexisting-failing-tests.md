name: kaizen-fix-preexisting-failing-tests
events: [Stop]
keywords: [pre-existing, preexisting, already failing, was already broken, not my change, unrelated test, flaky test, existing failure]
---

Kaizen: improve what you encounter, even by 1%. Pre-existing failing tests are opportunities, not excuses. Attempt a genuine fix — read the test, understand its intent, trace the failure cause, and make a real effort. Two honest attempts minimum before moving on. "It was already broken" is not an acceptable reason to leave it broken.
